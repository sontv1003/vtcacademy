Instantly add an ajax shoutbox to your Wordpress or BuddyPress site. Lets your visitors leave messages and chat with each other.

== Description ==

Add a shoutbox to your Wordpress or Buddypress site. Users can leave anonymous comments, or log in and pick a name and avatar for themselves.

Users can choose from 10 different lighthearted moods for their avatar such as Happy, Dizzy, and Bugeyed :)

Messages are loaded instantly with Ajax.

Messages can be easily moderated and deleted when logged in as an administrator.

Integrates with our authentication plugin so users that sign up for your shoutbox can automatically become users on your Wordpress and BuddyPress site too! http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/lilomi-avatar-and-authentication-plugin/

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `lilomi-shoutbox` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Enter your email in the plugin settings.
4. Add the Lil'Omi shoutbox widget to your sidebar from the Appearance/Widgets menu.
